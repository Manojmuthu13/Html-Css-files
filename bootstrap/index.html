about us

MassCoders, founded by Dodagatta Nihar in July 2023, is a visionary community with a mission to empower and inspire the youth to explore the world of coding. Our motto, "Motivating youth to code," encapsulates our dedication to making coding accessible and engaging for all, especially in native languages such as Telugu and expanding to Kannada.

What we do?

We specialize in providing coding tutorials in native languages, starting with Telugu, and extending our reach to Kannada. At MassCoders, we believe that learning to code should be inclusive and easy to understand, regardless of one's linguistic background.
